The Journey

7 Days of Sri Lanka

Day 1

Arrival & Relaxation

Location: Negombo / Kandana

Airport pickup and transfer to our comfortable homestay. Rest, recover, and gear up for the adventure ahead. Your private vehicle will be waiting for you here.

Day 2

The City of Gems

Location: Ratnapura

Dive into Sri Lanka's famous gem industry. We will visit active gem mines, observe traditional cutting and polishing, and explore an exclusive gem museum.

Day 3

Wild Safaris & Starry Nights

Location: Udawalawe

Morning safari in Udawalawe National Park, visit the Elephant Transit Home, hike the scenic Hawagala, and end the day with premium outdoor camping and food prepared by our team.

Day 4

The Hill Country

Location: Ella

Experience the world-renowned scenic train ride. Visit the iconic Nine Arch Bridge, bathe in Ravana Falls, and experience the spiritual calm of a local mountain temple.

Day 5

Trails & Waterfalls

Location: Horton Plains to Ratnapura

Trek to the dramatic World's End drop-off. Visit Sri Lanka's tallest waterfall, Bambarakanda. We will then head back to Ratnapura for an overnight stay.

Day 6

Urban Exploration & Sunset

Location: Colombo

Explore the vibrant capital. Ascend the Lotus Tower, visit the modern Port City, walk through the bustling Pettah Market, and relax at Mount Lavinia Beach at sunset.

Day 7

Departure

Location: Negombo Beach -> Airport

A final morning stroll along Negombo beach before we drop you safely at the airport, concluding your private duo adventure.
