The Journey

7 Days of Sri Lanka

Day 1

Arrival & Relaxation

Location: Negombo / Kandana

Airport pickup and transfer to our comfortable homestay. Rest, recover, and gear up for the adventure ahead. Your private vehicle will be waiting for you here.

Day 2

The City of Gems

Location: Ratnapura

Dive into Sri Lanka's famous gem industry. We will visit active gem mines, observe traditional cutting and polishing, and explore an exclusive gem museum.

Day 3

Wild Safaris & Starry Nights

Location: Udawalawe

Morning safari in Udawalawe National Park, visit the Elephant Transit Home, hike the scenic Hawagala mountain, and end the day with wild outdoor camping and food prepared by our team.

Day 4

The Hill Country

Location: Ella

Experience the world-renowned scenic train ride. Visit the iconic Nine Arch Bridge, see the majestic Ravana Falls (viewing only, no bathing), and experience the spiritual calm of a local mountain temple.

Day 5

Trails & Waterfalls

Location: Horton Plains to Ratnapura

Trek through the misty Horton Plains to the dramatic World's End drop-off. Visit Sri Lanka's tallest waterfall, Bambarakanda, and the beautiful Lanka Ella falls. We will then head back to Ratnapura for an overnight stay.

Day 6

Urban Exploration & Sunset

Location: Colombo

Explore the vibrant capital. Walk through the bustling Pettah Market, admire the Red Mosque, visit the modern Port City, and relax at Mount Lavinia Beach at sunset.

Day 7

Departure

Location: Negombo Beach -> Airport

A final morning stroll along Negombo beach before we drop you safely at the airport, concluding your private duo adventure.
